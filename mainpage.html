Our Training Classes

Beginner

"Starting from Basics"

The Basic plan is designed for beginners who are new to boxing or have limited experience. It focuses on building a strong foundation in boxing fundamentals, including proper stance, footwork, basic punches, and defensive techniques. The training sessions primarily focus on improving overall fitness, coordination, and introducing basic sparring skills. This plan is ideal for those who want to start their boxing journey and develop a solid understanding of the sport.

Intermediate

"Elevate next Level"

Our Intermediate plan is ideal for those with prior boxing exposure, eager to advance. It introduces advanced techniques, intricate combinations, and defensive skills. Training intensifies, targeting speed, power, and endurance improvement. Regular sparring sessions and skill evaluations refine technique, enhancing overall boxing abilities. Here this plan bridges foundational and advanced levels, guiding boxers toward well-rounded competence in the ring.

Advance

"Unleash full Potential"

Our Advanced plan caters to seasoned boxers aiming for competitive excellence. Tailored for those with a robust foundation, it delves into sophisticated boxing strategies, intricate combinations, and tactical prowess. Intense and rigorous training hones speed, agility, strength, and precision. Regular sparring sessions, skill evaluations, and specialized drills elevate performance, priming advanced boxers for professional bouts and competitive triumphs on the grand stage.
